History

Mr. Parvataneni Brahmayya, the founder of Brahmayya & Co., was a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of England and Wales who set up his practice in India at Chennai. Over the years, Brahmayya & Co. opened offices in several important locations across the country. Recognised for his role as one of the pioneers of the Chartered Accountancy practice in India, he was appointed as the President of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India in 1962. He also served as a Member of the Central Council of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India for five terms spanning fifteen years.

Ever since our inception, our mission has been to render professional services of the highest standard to clients, upholding the code of conduct and ethics of the profession.

With over 80 years of experience, excellence is a way of life at Brahmayya & Co. Our drive to deliver excellent services is entrenched in our deep-rooted commitment to our clients. Brahmayya & Co. takes pride in the long-standing relationships we have developed over the years with our clients, many of whom are market leaders in their respective fields.

Our motto ‘Experience, Excellence’ exemplifies our approach to value-oriented service delivery. Expert professionals ensure that our clients receive prompt and personalized solutions at all times. Brahmayya & Co. is helmed by experienced partners all over the country with commendable professional standing, experience and exposure in various fields associated with the Profession.
